About Me

I’m Francis Mwendwa with a strong background in cloud computing, network architecture, cybersecurity, and software development. With expertise in AWS, Python (Flask & Django), Terraform, and Docker, I specialize in building scalable cloud solutions, automating infrastructure, and securing networks.

Beyond cloud and network engineering, I enjoy researching emerging technologies, optimizing system performance, and mentoring aspiring tech professionals. My experience includes working with organizations such as the Ministry of Education (EMIS) and AICO, where I contributed to IT infrastructure and data-driven solutions.

Currently, I’m expanding my expertise in cybersecurity, learning about endpoint security, network defense, and ethical hacking, while also improving my skills in Dockerization and Kubernetes.
